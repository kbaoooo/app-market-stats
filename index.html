🔥 Insight #1
AI = New Default
AI is penetrating every category: fitness (AI coach 8%), books (AI summary 9%), education (AI tutor), photo (AI filter). Apps without AI features are becoming obsolete.
🔥 Insight #2
GP = Volume, iOS = Revenue
Google Play has 3.7x more downloads but only 42% of iOS revenue. Subscription monetization → iOS first. Ads/volume play → Google Play first.
🔥 Insight #3
30% = Real Opportunity
70–90% of searches are branded. The remaining 30% non-branded searches are where indie devs can actually compete: VPN, no wifi games, AI tools, photo editor, translator.
